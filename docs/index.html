Features

Everything your memory can't hold

♾️

Unlimited History

Every tab, app, and document — captured forever. No 90-day cutoff, no limits.

✨

AI Summaries & Tags

Powered by Apple Foundation Models — everything auto-summarised and tagged on-device.

🔍

Natural Language Search

"What was that article about protein folding I read last Tuesday?" — just ask.

📊

Statistics Dashboard

Visualise your app usage, top sites, and productivity patterns over time.

Export Your Data

Your memories, always portable. Export to JSON, CSV, or Markdown anytime.

AES-256-GCM Encryption

Everything encrypted at rest. Key lives in your Keychain. Zero access for us.
